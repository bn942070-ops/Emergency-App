🏥 About MedRush

MedRush is an emergency medical assistance platform that helps patients get immediate care by:

  • 🚑 Finding nearby hospitals based on your location
  • 🏥 Suggesting specialized hospitals based on symptoms
  • 📱 Sending real-time alerts to doctors before patient arrival
  • 🧭 Providing Google Maps navigation to hospitals
  • 📞 One-tap emergency call to 108 ambulance service

For Doctors

Doctors receive instant notifications when a patient is en route, allowing them to prepare for emergency care.

Emergency Numbers

📞 Ambulance: 108 | 🚓 Police: 100 | 🔥 Fire: 101
